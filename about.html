ABOUT US

NATCO Consulting recruits research assistants based on specific qualifications, often including a degree or background in research methods, data analysis, or a related field.

.

After hiring, the company typically implements an intensive training program that covers: 1. Research methodologies: Theoretical grounding in both qualitative and quantitative research techniques. 2. Data collection tools: Use of software or equipment necessary for gathering data, such as surveys, interviews, observation, or sensor-based technologies. 3. Ethics and compliance: Training on how to adhere to ethical standards and legal regulations in data collection, such as informed consent, data privacy, and confidentiality. 4. Fieldwork protocols: Guidelines on how to conduct surveys or interviews in the field, ensuring accuracy and reliability in data collection.

We aim at providing quality services to our research matters by ensuring that the research assistants are well trained and the data collected is of high quality.
